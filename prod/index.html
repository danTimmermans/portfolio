Daniel Timmermans

Front-end Junior Web developer

Hi there, It's Daniel,

for the time being I'm Front-end Junior Web developer learner at Digital City Brussels.

Former professional contrabassist & bass player I went from touring all over europe & creating jazz/prog rock & death metal bass lines to writing code lines, I'm also a syfy/horror & genre movies freak, a vinyl collector, a jogger & workout-aholic and a plant based cooking enthusiast .

During my learing I acquired strong basis in html/css, in javaScript with an introduction to Angular and with Wordpress by creating customs themes.

More about me

Now my goals are to implement everything I learned by joining a development agency, to perfect my knowledge and learning to become a successful professional front-end web developer.
